Since 1970, Ramees Restaurant has been a landmark of authentic Kerala cuisine. From our humble roots in Kollam — the Mutton Capital of Kerala — we have grown to serve guests across three cities, always staying true to our heritage of bold spices, fresh ingredients, and the warmth of a family table.
